Site Purpose

In my Country, during holidays a lot of people travel from cities to their home villages. This means very long queues at the bus terminals and long waiting hours before one can board a bus. The buses are often overloaded with seated and standing passengers and the journey lasts several hours, with no air conditioning. While those who travel in private cars have empty seats and are willing to give rides. My idea is to have a website where private car owners can connect with and give rides to other travelers headed in their direction. Fuel costs will be shared so both parties will benefit.

Intended Target Audience

General public in need of safer and more conducive ways of travel.

Why will people come to the site?

Those who wish to travel anywhere in the country will visit the website and check first if there is anyone headed to their destination. They will visit the website because it offers them an alternative or another choice.

What questions will visitors/users expect is answered by your site content?

Idealy for such a platform to work, users will need reasurance that fellow travellers are safe people to travel with. Hence a need for some identification and verification may later arise, users will need to create profiles with their background.

Why will visitors visit this site versus other sites of comparable purpose?

Ease of use and assurance of safety since all users will be verified.
