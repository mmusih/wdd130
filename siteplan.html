Site Name

Dry Oar river rafting provides the ultimate river rafting experience.

Site Purpose and Goals

The purpose of the website is to promote and advertise the services we offer, hence it should be visually appealing so that it converts visitors to customers.

Target Audience

Our audience is anyone who is above 5 years and is interested in rafting and looking for services. The general puplic is also our target, from 5years upwards, we want to appeal to and attract first timers. We want to;

  • Provide a convenient way to book
  • Provide a secure payment for services
  • Assure prospective customers of our safety high standards
  • Provide weather focust services so that users know what they weather will be like when they raft
